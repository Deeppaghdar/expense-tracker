Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and login credentials, along with the expense records you log — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to operate and improve Spendly: authenticating your account, storing your expenses, and generating the summaries and breakdowns you see in the app. We do not use your data for advertising and we do not sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely in our database and retained for as long as your account remains active. If you delete your account, your data is removed within a reasonable period, except where retention is required for legal or security reasons.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third party advertisers. We may rely on trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting and email delivery services) to operate the app, and these providers only process data as needed to provide their service to us.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided in your account settings.
